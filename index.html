About

The Golden Moment refers to the moment of clarity which results from sincere effort to expand personal awareness... allowing shifting perspectives where problems unravel and solutions unfold..where thinking and behaviors come into harmony with your life goals and purpose… this is what guides my practice.

I work with busy professionals, their spouses and families to harmonize goals with life purpose in order to achieve and maintain a healthy work/life balance. This absence of balance may lead to loss of direction and lack of purpose resulting in depressed mood, excessive anxiety, relationship issues and a general lack of comfort dealing with life’s ambiguities.

My therapeutic goal is to help you arrive at your golden moment by utilizing practical, efficient and purposeful interventions designed to expand insight; endowing you with clarity of purpose and direction to successfully navigate the paths which have been illuminated through our work together.

In addition to traditional methods of providing supportive care and cognitive behavioral therapy, I am also a certified EMDR therapist. EMDR stands for Eye Movement Desensitization and Reprocessing and offers a non-traditional approach to further address life-stress issues.

Intro

As a Licensed Professional Counselor I have been fortunate to assist clients addressing a wide variety of clinical issues across diverse treatment settings, by developing a relationship of trust while providing practical and compassionate insight. I collaborate with clients to expand their level of personal awareness by emphasizing the importance of creating and putting into practice more effective thinking, habits and behaviors. My ultimate therapeutic goal is to help facilitate positive change by bringing clients closer to the life they desire in an efficient and purposeful manner.
