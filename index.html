The Golden Moment

The Golden Moment refers to the moment of clarity which results from sincere effort to expand personal awareness... allowing shifting perspectives where problems unravel and solutions unfold..where thinking and behaviors come into harmony with your life goals and purposeâ€¦ this is what guides my practice.

My therapeutic goal is to help you arrive at your golden moment by utilizing practical, efficient and purposeful interventions designed to expand insight; endowing you with clarity of purpose and direction to successfully navigate the paths which have been illuminated through our work together.

In addition to traditional methods of providing supportive care and cognitive behavioral therapy, I am also a certified EMDR therapist. EMDR stands for Eye Movement Desensitization and Reprocessing and offers a non-traditional approach to further address life-stress issues.

Introduction

Christopher DuFour, MA, LPC

Certified EMDR Therapist

As a Licensed Professional Counselor, I bring a wealth of knowledge and multifaceted perspective in supporting individuals desiring to make a substantive change in their lives. Having worked in the financial services industry for 14 years as an investment advisor / financial planner, I saw the impact of competent counsel when combined with a supportive, trusting relationship. This motivated me to pursue my goal of becoming a therapist.

Since becoming a Licensed Professional Counselor, I have been fortunate to assist clients in a wide variety of settings by developing a relationship of trust and providing practical, yet compassionate insight. I collaborate with clients to expand their level of personal awareness by emphasizing the importance of creating and putting into practice more effective thinking, habits and behaviors. My ultimate therapeutic goal is to help facilitate positive change to bring clients closer to the life they desire in an efficient and purposeful manner.
